I am a California licensed Doctor of Psychology with a certificate in Coaching and Psychotherapy. For the past 10 years, I have successfully worked with a diverse group of people including medical professionals, teachers, engineers, scientists, executives, small business owners, high school, college and graduate students, parents, and more.

Before I became a therapist and coach, I spent time at Accenture as a web developer and process consultant. At the YMCA, I managed special events and fundraising activities. I have been married for 13 years, have twin boys and a little girl. I also have spent a combined 21 years studying dance and yoga, both of which have taught me immensely about mind-body connection and integration.

My own life has been about facing challenges successfully and learning to live Abundantly. I understand intimately why you are searching for answers and solutions, especially given the culturally diverse and high-stress environment of Silicon Valley. You need practical solutions for your unique challenges.
