    CANCELLATION POLICY
    I have a 48-hour cancellation policy. If you do not call to cancel your reserved appointment or reschedule within this time period, you will be charged the full appointment fee.
  • In addition to your session time, my fee covers time spent in between appointments on various supplemental tasks such as: phone calls, research for other resources you may need, reviewing your progress, preparing needed paperwork, and most importantly, considering how to best help you. I accept checks or cash, internet-based bank direct pay transfers, and credit cards with a convenience fee. I have a select number of sliding scale fee slots available. Please call me to discuss fees, service packages, and flexible payment options.
  • I am considered an Out-of-Network provider for all insurance plans. If you have a PPO plan, I will bill your insurance company on your behalf to reduce your administrative overhead. Please email me for an insurance benefits worksheet to clarify your plan’s fee reimbursements. Mental health coverage varies by plan. Please be aware your insurance company will require several weeks to process your receipts for reimbursement.
